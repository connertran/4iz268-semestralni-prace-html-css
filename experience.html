STARTUP

OneNine company

CTO/ Fullstack developer

June 2024 - Present

OneNine company is my startup company that me and my co-founder founded in 2024.

The problem we see right now is that now AI language models work very well with high resource languages, such as English, Spanish, Chinese, etc. But for low resource languages, such as Swahili, Vietnamese, and other unpopular languages in Asia, Africa or in other countries, AI language models don't work well, and still need rooms for improvement.

The fact is AI now can work with 100+ languages. However, we have more than 7000 languages, which mean many people, who don't speak high resource languages, like English are left behind.

We don't want anyone to be left behind, and we see other companies are trying to improve AI language models for high language model even more, and haven't focused in this area yet. Therefore, we decided to tackle in this area and to be one of the pioneers in this area.

At OneNine, we do not train AI models, but we collect data from different low-resource languages, and then we provide/supply the prepared/labeled data to big AI companies, such as OpenAI, Google, Meta, etc.

Projects

Flowy - SaaS business

Back in the days, I wanted to scale my personal brand on X (a.k.a. Twitter). However, I found that is was challenging to come up with new ideas and post them on the platform, because in order to grow your folowers you need to be active on X.

I asked myself: "With all my fullstack skills, how can I build a software that can solve my own problem? Hmm..., what about other people, who also have the similar problem like me? Struggling to manage their time in a day to be active on X? Let's build a SaaS business..."

Therefore, I dived into the idea and built Flowy, a software that helps people generate new ideas for posts powered by AI. The application also provides users with automatic posting feature. That means, users can focus on their business, and let the software handle from generating content to posting them on X for them daily, or based on their schedule.

I am so greatful, that people are using my product. What makes me even more happy is. Users often require new features or updates. This means that my product has a big impact on their daily workflow.

If you also have the similar problem like us, trying to grow on X, but struggle to find the right time in a day to do it. Please check out Flowy. I hope it will make your life easier.
